## ‚ú® Key Features
### 1. Interactive Experience
- **Dynamic Typing Text**: A hero section animation cycling through professional roles (Python Developer, Data Engineer, etc.).
- **Smooth Navigation**: One-page layout with smooth scrolling and active link highlighting based on scroll position.
- **Interactive Timeline**: A detailed career history with hover effects and scroll-based reveal animations.
- **Statistical Counters**: Animated counters for years of experience, microservices developed, and projects completed.

### 2. Modern Design & UX
- **Responsive Layout**: Fully optimized for mobile, tablet, and desktop viewing.
- **Micro-Animations**: Custom scroll reveal animations for sections, hover-based project overlays, and floating background shapes.
- **Glassmorphism & Gradients**: Modern aesthetic using semi-transparent elements and subtle gradients for a premium feel.
- **Parallax Hero**: A subtle parallax effect on the landing section for depth.
- **Visitor Tracker**: A live hit counter in the footer powered by `hits.sh`.

### 3. Content Sections
- **About Me**: Professional summary and key technical stats.
- **Skills Grid**: Organized categorization of tech stacks (Backend, Databases, AI/ML, Cloud/DevOps, Architecture).
- **Featured Projects**: Portfolio of major work with technology tags and direct links.
- **Interests**: Personal and professional areas of focus (Computer Vision, NLP, System Design).
- **Contact System**: Integration of contact information (GitHub, LinkedIn) and a functional front-end message form.
